    Main Characters


Yumeko Jabami

Jabami Yumeko is a black long-haired girl.
She enters a private school namedHyakkaou Private Academy which looks normal at first glance, but the school is full of gambling where students are ranked by their gambling winnings, and fame and fortune awaits those at the top.




Ryota Suzui

Ryota Suzui is one of the protagonists and a former housepet student of Hyakkou Private Academy whom he became after losing to Mary Saotome in a gambling match.

He is a kind and friendly person, although not necessarily outspoken or reserved. He's also shown to be timid, rarely ever showin any form of confidence. But after meeting Jabami Yumeko, his self-confidence slowly grows.



Jun Kiwatari

Jun Kiwatari goes to Hyakkaou Academy and a minor antagonist.

He often targeted new housepets and tormented them in order to break them, so he could use them for his own benefit later. When Nanami became a housepet, he escorted her out and cut her hair, even though she begged him not to. Jun compared it to cutting one's nails if they get to long and how it feels so good. Jun also bullies Ryota frequently, while he was a housepet



Mary Saotome

A student of Hyakkaou Private Academy and also a classmate Yumeko Jabami and Ryota Suzui. At first, Mary is shown to be incredibly sadistic and twisted. One example of her personality is how she treats her classmate Ryota Suzui after he turned into a housepet due to his low social status within the academy. After losing to Yumeko and experiencing the life of a housepet, she bevame desperate to regain her status.



Itsuki Sumeragi

Is a first year student Hyakkaou Private Academy is a member of the student council and her father is the president of a prominent toy company in Japan.





Kirari Momobami

She is the major antagonist and the 105th student council president at Hyakkaou Private Academy. She is also responsible for the current heirarchy at the academy.

She may look like a refined and proper lady on the outside, but she is in truth a calculating, manipulative, psychopathic individual with little regard for anything other than herself.



Kaede Manyuda

Kaede Manyuda is a supporting character. He is the manipulative former Treasurer of the student council who has his own goal to take down Kirari Momobami.

Kaede appears to be a cold, ruthless and calculating person who is an extremely prudent and observant gambler, as he always takes the high road, which he himself often notes. His mindfulness allows for him to predict his opponent's actions and he often wins due to this.
