Let's Get Our Gambling Freak On!

Kakegurui is an anime about a girl entering a school full of gambling.
The main character, Jabami Yumeko, being a gambling prodigy, wants to gamble againts the student council. But to gamble with the student council, she had to fight some of the student council.


She meets Suzui where he sometimes help her with her gambles. She also meets Mary, her classmate, in the classroom and begins a gamble.


The first season aired from July to September 2017 and the second season was aired to January to March 2019. Kakegurui Twin is a prequel of the first season Kakegurui: Compulsive Gambler and it's sequel, Kakegurui XX.
